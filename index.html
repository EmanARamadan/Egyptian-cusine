Explore the taste of Egyptian Cusine!

For millennia, ancient Egypt has intrigued the minds of many. "Egyptian Cusine" featured curated stories that highlight how dishes like feseekh (sun-dried and salted fish) were inherited over centuries and generations. The ancient Egyptians were diligent about documentation — hence the inscribed recipes on tombs that feature ingredients that are a staple in the modern-day Egyptian kitchen. The online project also reveals that what were relatively new ingredients in the past — such as aubergine and pasta — influenced dishes in Egyptian modern-day cuisine, like koshary and taameya.
